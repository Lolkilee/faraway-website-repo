About Faraway

A Sci-fi low poly RTS game.
‍
Together with your enemies, you are placed in the universe of Faraway. The player is set as a leader of a people, who, without the player's guidance, would fall under the might of the opposing civilizations.

The Player starts out with a single planet, chosen by the player at the beginning of the game.
The Player will have to build up a space-faring civilization from the start, by colonizing other planets and exploiting the local asteroid belts to gather resources.

However, the player is not alone in the galaxy, as opposing civilization also seek to conquer the galaxy. With similar interests and not enough resources to fit everybody, conflict is inevitable in the galaxy of Faraway, where the flame of war rages throughout the galaxy.

Exploration

‍The Universe of Faraway is procedurally generated, resulting in endless opportunities to discover new lands and colonize new land. However, not all planets are created equal, meaning that some might be more beneficial to the player, while others might prove to be useless.

Construction

‍Of course, a galaxy cannot be
explored without the help of technology, and so a such the player will have to build their civilization the tools to explore the galaxy, both military and non-military.

To do this, the player will have to gather resources by exploiting their colonized planets or create specialized crafts, capable of extracting resources from the cores of asteroids, which can be found in neighbouring asteroid fields.

Combat

With opposing civilizations seeking to claim resources, from themselves, the player has to build a space navy to defend their own resources or seek to claim resources from opponents.

With dynamic space based, combat Faraway seeks to encourage different tactics and have multiple options for the player to choose from.
